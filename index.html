ABOUT UNIVERSITY

Mizoram University was created by an Act of Parliament (No. 8 of 2000) and it became functional from 2nd July, 2001. The University is located at Aizawl, the capital city of Mizoram and is spread over 978.1988 acres in an area on the outskirts of the city. Prior to the existence of Mizoram University, the North-Eastern Hill University (NEHU) with headquarters at Shillong was operating a campus in Mizoram. Headed by a Pro-Vice Chancellor, the then Mizoram Campus of NEHU consisted of seven Post-Graduate Departments, namely, Economics, Education, English, Forestry, Mizo, Psychology and Public Administration. During the last 22 years of its existence, Mizoram University has made considerable progress in terms of infrastructure, academic programmes, manpower and support services


ABOUT SCHOOL OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in the year 2011 under the School of Engineering and Technology (SET) and the department is conducting 4 years Bachelor of technology (B.tech) course in Computer Engineering with an intake capacity of 30 students. The first batch was started in the month of August 2012 and subsequent batches are started thereafter. Out of 30 intake, 15 students are taken through JEE main and 15 student are taken through MZUEEE (Mizoram University Engineering Entrance Examination). There are three (03) computer labs with 30 or more computers. All class rooms are equipped with ICT Facilities. The department has dedicated state of the art high performance Computing Laboratory with 1:1 computer student ratio guided by Teacher and technical staff. The primary focus is to impart quality education in the field of Computer Science & Engineering with Practical knowledge. The students will have opportunities to gain industrial exposure through industrial programme offered in the course.


ABOUT CONFERENCE

The ICAIRET is expected to create meeting point in engineering education for skilled professionals to interact and discuss cutting edge research and recent developments for digital India. It encourages and assists the professionals engaged in various technological engineering fields to maintain the integrity and competence of the profession. It fosters a sense of partnership among the professionals through research in state of the art methodologies within its areas of expertise for improving the common mans life by developing technical tools or models or products of their needs in creating digital India. It also serves as a platform for innovative researchers who can share their experiences, exchange their realistic ideas and solutions for the implementing problems faced in the society. It is a premier platform for the students, researchers, emeritus, professional and industry practitioners to share their new and innovative ideas, original research finding and practical development experiences in engineering through high quality peer reviewed papers.

PAPER SUBMISSION

Submissions of high quality papers in scope of conference are handled only through the CMT website at


https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICONIC2024/Submission/Index

Review Process

  1. The submissions are generally reviewed for relevance with the conference scope, novelty against current state of the art, significance of the research contribution and English language.
  2. All submitted papers will be checked for plagarism report using Turnitin. If the paper contains more than 15% plagarism , review process will not be carried.
  3. ICAIRET research papers are reviewed using a Single -blind review process managed through CMT. Each paper is reviewed independently by two reviewers, and assigned reviewers submit their anonymous reviews through CMT.
  4. ICAIRET TPC then select the papers for conference proceeding based on reviewers score and comments.

CALL FOR PAPERS


International Conference on Advanced Innovative Research in Engineering and Technology (ICAIRET 24) conference is an open forum for the exchange of state-of-the-art knowledge concerning Engineering and Technology. The conference solicits papers on all aspects of Intelligent Computing and Machine Learning, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Food Technology and application deployment. The conference will witness multiple eminent keynote speakers from academia and industry from all over the world along with the presentation of accepted peer-reviewed articles.

The topics covered (but are not limited to) in the conference are as follows:

  Robotics
  Intelligent data analysis
  Artificial intelligence (AI)
  Privacy and Security

Track 2 : Data Communication and Computer Networking

  Internet of Things & Cyber Physical Systems

  Cryptography & Network Security

Track 3 : Electrical Engineering
